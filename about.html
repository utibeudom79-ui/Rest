Our Story

Born from a Love of
African Flavours

Ember & Spice was founded in 2014 in the heart of Uyo, Akwa Ibom, by a family with a deep love for authentic Nigerian cuisine. What started as a small kitchen serving neighbourhood meals has grown into a beloved destination for food lovers across the city.

We believe food is more than nourishment — it is culture, memory, and connection. From our smoky Jollof Rice to our rich Edikang Ikong, every plate tells a story rooted in tradition and love.

Our Mission

Serving with Soul

To preserve and celebrate African culinary heritage by delivering bold, authentic, and unforgettable dining experiences — one plate at a time.
